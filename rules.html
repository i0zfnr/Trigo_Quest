Sine & Cosine Rules

1.3.1 Sine Rule

Sine Rule can used in any triangle (not just right-angled triangles) where a side and its opposite angle are known :

Finding sides: a / sin A = b / sin B = c / sin C

Finding angles: sin A / a = sin B / b = sin C / c

Requires one pair of a side and its opposite angle.

1.3.2 Cosine Rule

Cosine Rule can useed inany triangle where you are trying to relate all three sides to one angle :

Finding sides: a² = b² + c² – 2bc cos A

Finding angles: cos A = (b² + c² – a²) / (2bc)

1.3.3 Area of Triangle (Trigonometric Form)

Area of triangle trig is a formula to calculate the area of any triangle

Area = ½ ab sin C

Use when you know two sides and the included angle.
